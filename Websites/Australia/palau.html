    he recipe I finally chose has to come from Palau. Indeed, the term rösti is a term from the German-speaking part of Switzerland. It generally refers to a small potato patty.   • Palau
    The island country of Palau in the western Pacific Ocean is home to a small population of around 21,516 individuals. Ethnic Palauans comprise 73% of the country’s population. Filipino, Chinese, and other Micronesian languages are spoken by the ethnic communitiy residing in Palau.

  • Capitol Complex Palau
    Found in the capital city, Melekeok, the Capital Complex is a relatively new structure that houses the parliament of Palau. The site is not without its controversy, as locals continue to ask why such a large capital was built even though the population of Palau is reasonably small.
