Preface

Why did we start the project "WorldTour"? We are three students of the HTBLA Leonding and "WorldTour" was THE idea for our one-year school project. Together we decided to create a website that provides an information-rich overview of all countries in the world. By our experience, it is often time-consuming to acquire enough subject areas and, above all, the correct information needed for travel, school projects, presentations or simply as knowledge acquisition. With this in mind, we have made the effort to create a clear and diverse website.

So what about a trip around the world? Sit back and enjoy the "WorldTour". Discover new countries, cities, recipes, cultures and much more! - The best part? You can experience the adventure at any time in any location. To learn more about the upcoming world we want to give you the oppertunity to a tour which you can find when you scroll a bit further down the page. Have fun with our exclusive "WorldTour"!

2D-Map

Expand your knowledge and click through the exclusive 2D-map. By clicking on the various countries of all the nations of the world, you will be taken to a websites where you can learn more about the specific country in short and entertaining videos, some facts, as well as the geographical location. The "WorldTour" gives you the opportunity to try international recipes and learn about cultures, religions, dances, rituals, traditional clothing and more.
