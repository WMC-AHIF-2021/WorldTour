  • Pastellaki
    Cypriot pastelli is a traditional sweet prepared from carob syrup, which is boiled down to a sticky substance that can be molded by hand into the shape in which it is then commercialized. Go and try it out!
    - Recipes

  • Cyprus
    The Mediterranean island of Cyprus has a culture that is a blend of many cultures. The Greek Cypriots and Turkish Cypriots share many cultural aspects together but also have a lot of several differences.
    - Culture

  • Aphrodite’s Rock
    Ancient tradition marks this unique offshore rock formation as the birthplace of Aphrodite, Greek goddess of love and fertility. Many pictures are often used as backgrounds, so go and visit this incredible attraction.

Mediterranean Sea

The Mediterranean Sea is a sea connected to the Atlantic Ocean, surrounded by the Mediterranean Basin and almost completely enclosed by land: on the north by Western and Southern Europe and Anatolia, on the south by North Africa, and on the east by the Levant. Cyprus is surrounded by the Mediterranean Sea.

Turkey

Turkey, is a country located mainly on Anatolia in Western Asia, with a portion on the Balkans in Southeast Europe. It shares borders with Greece and Bulgaria, the Black Sea, Georgia, Armenia, Azerbaijan, and Iran to the east, Iraq, Syria, the Mediterranean Sea and the Aegean Sea.

Syria

Syria is a country in Western Asia. It borders the Mediterranean Sea to the west, Turkey to the north, Iraq to the east and southeast, Jordan to the south, and Israel and Lebanon to the southwest. Cyprus lies to the west across the Mediterranean Sea.
