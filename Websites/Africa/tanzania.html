  • Mandazi


    Mandazi is the term used to define a fried dough in the Kiswahili language. Andazi is the word used in the singular form while the word mandazi is plural to define these African donuts. Ingredients traditionally used to prepare mandazi include flour, egg, sugar, spices, and not to mention the coconut milk.

    - Recipes

  • Culture - Tanzania


    A slew of festivals go on throughout the year in Tanzania featuring traditional dress and dance, such as the Wanyambo Festival in Dar es Salaam and the Mwaka Kogwa Festival in Zanzibar. There are also periodic arts and film events, while objects of fascination can be picked up at markets nationwide.

    - Culture

  • Special Sights


    National Parks, mountains and tropical beaches are Tanzania’s main draws, with the savannahs of the Serengeti plains right up there as some of the most spectacular places on earth. Big game animals call the region home and there is no better place to get a safari. The nearby Ngorongoro Crater is also spellbinding.
