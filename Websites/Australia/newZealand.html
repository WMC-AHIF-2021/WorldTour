  • New Zealand
    The island of New Zealand is located in the southwestern region of the Pacific Ocean, southeast of Australia. New Zealand is a diverse nation, with colonial Western culture influenced by Māori customs and traditions. Before the 1800s, the Māori pre-colonial culture was dominant on the island.

  • Fiordland National Park
    If only visiting one of New Zealand’s 14 national parks, Fiordland National Park on the western coast of the South Island is the biggest and the best. There are some incredible hiking and walking trails to be found and you could spend days exploring, kayaking, fishing, hunting, and climbing.
