  • Fish Curry

  • Salomon-Island
    The sovereign island country of the Solomon Islands hosts a population of around 660,121 individuals. The country has six major islands and more than 900 smaller islands. Honiara is located on Guadalcanal Island's northwestern coast.
    - Culture

  • Honiara
    Honiara is the capital and largest city of the Solomon Islands, situated on the northwestern coast of Guadalcanal. As of 2021, it had a population of 92,344 people. The city is served by Honiara International Airport and the seaport of Point Cruz.

Papua New Guinea

Papua New Guinea is a country in Oceania that comprises the eastern half of the island of New Guinea and its offshore islands in Melanesia.

Vanuatu

Vanuatu is an island country located in the South Pacific Ocean. Vanuatu was first inhabited by Melanesian people.

Nauru

Nauru, formerly known as Pleasant Island, is an island country and microstate in Oceania, in the Central Pacific. Its nearest neighbour is Banaba Island in Kiribati, 300 km to the east. It further lies northwest of Tuvalu, 1,300 km northeast of Solomon Islands, east-northeast of Papua New Guinea, southeast of the Federated States of Micronesia and south of the Marshall Islands.
