  • Sukuma Wiki


    In Kenya, the leafy plant, colewort is more commonly called sukuma wiki, and is often referred to as collard greens. This dish is full of nutrients and the carbohydrates mean that you are satisfied and stay full for longer time.
    Simple and cheap to make, yet tasty, satisfying and healthy, this is one dish that will definitely be revisited!

  • Culture - Kenya


    Kenya is a culturally diverse nation made up of different tribal groups, each with distinct languages, dress, music, and food. Some of the better known tribes include the coastal Swahili people and Maasai warriors in the wildlife rich grasslands. As much as a quarter of the population belongs to farming communities in the north.

    - Culture

  • Special Sights


    The place to go for a safari, Kenya is known for the “big five”– elephants, lions, leopards, rhinos, and buffalo.

    Big game hunting has largely given way to the more eco-friendly wildlife safaris that have helped Kenya become one of Africa’s most popular tourist destinations.
